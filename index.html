About Our Mission

Making a Difference

The Sungai Klang, one of Malaysia's most important rivers, faces severe pollution challenges. Our project aims to clean, restore, and protect this vital waterway for future generations.

Our Goals:

  • Remove plastic and waste from the river
  • Restore native aquatic ecosystem
  • Raise environmental awareness
  • Create sustainable solutions
